Our Story

About Company

Starting from humble beginnings in Seberang Jaya in October 2016, Sweet Treats now has over 105 outlets across Malaysia and is poised to rapidly expand in 2022. Sweet Treats success stems from a founding team with diverse experience in not only food, but also social media and real estate – which explains Sweet Treats active fanbase online (with close to 500,000 combined on all our Facebook & Instagram pages!) as well as ensuring the outlets launched new high traffic & suitable locations that enjoy an active repeat customer base that can be seen, even just an hour before closing time.

We are grateful to our discerning customers who appreciate our no-compromise approach, which includes premium ingredients, beautiful art products, tasteful decor, and, of course, first-rate service. 

Sweet Treats

During their travels abroad, the founders of Sweet Treats discovered a unique Japanese pastry, a fish-shaped snack made of croissant with a variety of sweet and savoury fillings such as red bean, chocolate banana, and cheese. He then made the necessary preparations, such as learning how to make it by hand, acquiring the signature mould and ingredients that give the Taiyaki its distinct shape and flavour, and launching with authentic Japanese ranges before expanding them to suit local tastes.

Not satisfied with pastries alone, he set out to make Sweet Treats the epitome of a 21st-century Japanese dessert house, adding signature toasts and shaved ice desserts (Kakigori) that incorporate fresh fruits, ice cream, and layers of flavour. To those looking for healthier options, there's also the Kenko Kakigori, a guilt-free option that incorporates shaved ice, yogurt / milk, fresh fruits, and a variety of grains.

Sweet Treats is humbled, grateful, and encouraged by the support we've received from both old and new customers, as well as the constant inquiries from potential business partners and licensees.
