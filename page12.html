Hi, how can we help?

Are your product halal?

Sweet Treats desserts are proudly Halal certified to the highest standard from the Department of Islamic Development of Malaysia (JAKIM).

For more info, refer www.halal.gov.my

What is Delivery Service?

You can now order in advance for your favorite dessert and beverage! Get them delivered to your homes via GrabFood and FoodPanda.

What are the benefits?

Skip the Queue, Cashless Payment, Save Time, Collect Rewards & Earn Points, Exclusive Promo

How much does the Delivery charge?

Applicable delivery charges will depend upon the location of the delivery address. You can check the delivery charges for your order during checkout.

Payment method?

Sweet Treats accepts both debit/credit cards (Visa & Master), FPX, E-Wallet (TnG, Grabpay, Maybank, Boost, Shopee and etc.)

Others?

If you have further questions which are not listed above, please contact us via email.
